# Code of Conduct

Open Intelligence is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful in language and tone
- Giving and receiving feedback constructively
- Assuming good intent while staying accountable

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Personal attacks, trolling, or sustained disruption
- Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.

## Reporting

To report a violation, contact: **security@openintelligence.ai**  
Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with care.
