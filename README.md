# Open Intelligence

Open Intelligence is an AI research and product company building lifelong AI companions that help people think, remember, create, and grow.

## Mission

Build trustworthy, personal intelligence that compounds over time and stays aligned with human goals.

## Products

### PAL  
**Personal Artificial Life Companion** — a persistent AI companion designed to understand you over time and support daily life.

### LifeGraph  
A privacy-respecting memory and relationship graph that organizes your context, preferences, and history.

### LifeOS  
An intelligent operating layer that orchestrates tasks, tools, and personal workflows across your digital life.

## Engineering Principles

- Build for long-term reliability, not short-term demos.
- Prefer simple, composable systems over fragile complexity.
- Design for safety, observability, and iteration from day one.
- Treat product quality, research rigor, and user trust as inseparable.

## Privacy-First Philosophy

- Personal data belongs to the user.
- Privacy is a default, not an optional setting.
- Minimize data collection and retain only what is necessary.
- Be transparent about how data is used and protected.
