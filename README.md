# Open Intelligence

### Building AI that grows with people.

Artificial intelligence today is designed to answer questions.

We believe the next generation of AI should understand context, remember what people choose to share, and grow alongside people throughout their lives.

Open Intelligence is building that future through privacy-first AI products designed to help people think, remember, create, and grow.

---

# Mission

Build trustworthy, personal intelligence that compounds over time and stays aligned with human goals.

---

# Our Philosophy

We believe intelligence is more than answering questions.

It is built through context, relationships, experiences, and time.

We are building AI that:

* Understands rather than simply responds.
* Remembers what people choose to share.
* Respects privacy by default.
* Grows alongside every person throughout their lifetime.
* Helps people become more organized, informed, and capable.

---

# Products

## PAL

**Personal Artificial Life Companion**

PAL is a lifelong AI companion designed to understand people through shared experiences, memories, and context.

Rather than acting as a chatbot, PAL evolves over time to become a trusted companion that helps people organize, remember, create, and navigate daily life.

---

## LifeGraph

LifeGraph is Open Intelligence's contextual memory engine.

It organizes relationships, places, documents, events, goals, habits, and experiences into a structured understanding of a person's life, enabling PAL to reason with context instead of isolated conversations.

---

## LifeOS

LifeOS is our long-term vision for an AI-native operating system.

Instead of treating AI as another application, LifeOS integrates intelligence into the foundation of computing, enabling every interaction to become more personal, contextual, and useful.

---

# Engineering Principles

* Build for long-term reliability, not short-term demos.
* Prefer simple, composable systems over fragile complexity.
* Design for safety, observability, and continuous iteration.
* Treat product quality, research rigor, and user trust as inseparable.
* Every feature should improve the user's life without compromising privacy.

---

# Privacy First

Privacy is not a feature.

It is the foundation of Open Intelligence.

We believe:

* Personal data belongs to the user.
* Privacy should be enabled by default.
* Data collection should always be transparent.
* Users remain in complete control of what is remembered, forgotten, and shared.
* AI should work for people—not the other way around.

---

# Long-Term Vision

We believe the future of computing will not be defined by applications alone.

It will be defined by intelligence that understands people over time.

Our vision is to build an ecosystem where:

* PAL becomes your lifelong AI companion.
* LifeGraph becomes your personal knowledge and memory engine.
* LifeOS becomes the operating system built around human intelligence rather than applications.
